Take your business

to the next level

Dátil is a mobile point of sale that fits your business model and helps you to increase productivity from the power of your fingers.

Billing and invoice electronically

Make and send invoices to your customers emails without printing.

More possibilities for your clients

Bill with cash, credit card or e-money from your mobile phone.

Take control of your products

Organize all your items in lists with price and tax details.

Learn more about your business

Get statistics and valuable information about your sales, products and customers.

Make your sales in a easier way.

The application enables you to execute any product sale in easy steps that take just a few taps with your finger.

Choose your products from your list, they will be stored in the shopping cart and confirm the sale with your customer with a payment method and presto!

Manage your invoices like never before

DATIL has a electronic invoice system for a integral experience for your clients at shopping time.

They will receive your invoices via email in a secure way and complying with the 'Servicio de Rentas Internas' requirements.

More possibilities for your clients.

Give your clients more alternatives at the payment process and they will have a lovely shopping experience. The platform allows you to make and register cash or credit card payments.

Futhermore, we provide the new system for e-money payments powered by 'The Central Bank of Ecuador', a secure, easy-to-use and market-cheap payment method. Thus, your clients will have a bunch of payment options at shopping time.

Take total control of your products.

Create an organized and detailed list of all your products to manage your business more easily and order.

Add articles with name, color, price and a photo, then they will be quickly identified when you are making a sale.

DATIL in my smartphone.

Even the simpler smartphone has the possibility DATIL without any inconvenience.

The application is developed to work in Android phones and coming soon to iOS and tablets.
